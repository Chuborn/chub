[Slinky]
Client version: 1.7 / 1.8.9 (injectable)
Price: $75/year ($50 on sale) (subscription) - slinky.gg
Client focus: Ghost / Hybrid client - Universal bypasses
Update Schedule: good
[combat modules]
Slinky has some of the best combat modules. It's backtrack for exemple, is both very strong and very customisable, allowing you to make an undetectable yet powerful config. It also includes some semi blatant - blatant modules with a working blatant autoblock, a "silent aimassist" which works as a killaura. Craziest of all, it features an inventory move and invmanager module with multiple option to bypass hypixel and others.
[visuals]
While Slinky's visuals are not lacking and rich in number and options, and while I am a big fan of the clickgui, I have heard and seen criticism of the visuals which I think it is fair to mention.
[personal opinion]
I am a fan. Slinky is my go-to ghost client, having in my opinion the best modules there is. The fact I can also go full blatant with it is also something I love, be it to toggle up or simply chill a bit. Now, slinky is not perfect, the biggest "issue" being of course the Price. At $50 on sale for a year, Slinky is the most expensive client there is. If you cannot afford Slinky, I thus orient you toward vape
, which I might not love but that still is probably the best there is on a budget
