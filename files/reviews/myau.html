[Myau client]
Client version: 1.8.9 (forge mod)
ViaVersion Support: With 3rd party mod

Client focus: Hypixel

[hypixel bypasses]
Myau, despite it's slow update schedule, has strong hypixel bypasses. It features one of the strongest killaura and a very good scaffold. It does not however has bypassses such as a full inventory move or a sword noslow; Thus, while Myau is one of the strongest client for Hypixel, it also has a more minimalist approach, both in term of visuals and in term of bypasses.
[multiserver bypasses]
Myau has not been made with multiserver in mind. It does however, thanks to it's good hypixel bypasses, have modules that can be used on servers that feature GRIM anticheat and other prediction based anticheats. It is not made for it, but know that it is decent on those
Myau also is a hybrid client and while I don't think it should be mainly used as such, it is a decent options to ghost cheat on a multitude of servers and toggle if needed. It being a forge mod means it can be easily combined with a pvp modpack if your choice
[personal opinion]
I am personally a big fan of Myau. I like it's minimalist approach and it's the go-to client when I want to have a good time without loss. It is however unfortunate that it has such a lacking update schedule because there not being things such as a sword noslow really is a downer. This slows down the already slow prediction gameplay and while I wouldn't call it a dealbreaker, it definitly is something to keep in mind.
