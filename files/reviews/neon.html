[hypixel bypasses]
Neon currently features strong hypixel bypasses such as a decent autoblock and one of the best inventory move. It's scaffold is decent tho it does lack a telly tower and it is not a "strong point" of the client. Nontheless, it does the job and the client itself works fine on hypixel
[multiserver bypasses]
Neon features extremely customisable modules. This, together with the many already integrated bypass, make the client a solid choice for multiserver. Being a modern client, it also features many bypasses for cubecraft including a fly, a longjump I love and a nofall
[personal opinion]
